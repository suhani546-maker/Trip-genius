Travel planning, reimagined.

No spreadsheets. No generic templates. Just a trip built around you.

The Problem

Most travel apps give every user the same itinerary for the same destination — regardless of their budget, interests, or pace. Planning a trip still means hours of cross-referencing attractions, checking travel times, and building manual spreadsheets. And the interface never changes, whether you're planning a snowy mountain retreat or a beach getaway.

What TripGenius Does

You tell us your destination, budget, trip duration, interests, and preferred travel pace. Our AI engine generates a realistic, day-by-day itinerary — with timing, activities, and costs tailored specifically to you. No two users planning the same destination get the same plan, because no two preferences are the same.

Adaptive UI

The interface itself responds to where you're going. Plan a trip to a beach destination and the page shifts into warm coastal tones. Choose a mountain retreat and the theme transforms into cool, icy visuals. Pick an urban getaway and the design adapts into a sleek city aesthetic. The same AI that generates your itinerary also classifies the character of your destination — no fixed list of cities, no hardcoding.

How It Works

Behind the scenes, TripGenius runs on a Node.js and Express backend, with MongoDB storing your trips securely. When you submit a trip, your preferences are sent to an AI model that simultaneously classifies the destination's landscape and constructs a personalized, hour-by-hour schedule — balancing your budget, interests, and pace in real time.
